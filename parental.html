About the Portal

The Parental Engagement Portal is a web-based platform designed to engage and educate parents about their child's education. The portal provides resources for supporting learning at home, tracking student progress, and receiving updates from teachers.

Features

  • Parent-Teacher Communication: Easily communicate with teachers to discuss your child's progress and needs.
  • Educational Resources: Access a variety of resources to help support your child's learning at home.
  • Progress Reports: Track your child's academic progress with detailed reports and insights.
  • Event Notifications: Stay informed about school events, meetings, and important dates.
