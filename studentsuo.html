About the App

The Student Support and Engagement App is designed to provide personalized support and engagement for students at risk of dropping out. With features like counseling support, motivational content, and tools for setting and tracking academic goals, this app aims to address the key factors contributing to student attrition.

Features

  • Counseling Resources: Access to professional counseling support and resources to help students navigate challenges.
  • Goal-Setting Tools: Tools to help students set, track, and achieve their academic goals.
  • Motivational Content: Inspirational articles, videos, and quotes to keep students motivated and engaged.
  • Engagement Tracking: Monitor student engagement and progress to identify at-risk students early.
